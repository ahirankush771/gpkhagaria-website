Admission Process

Simple 4-step process to join GP Khagaria.

1

Check Eligibility

10th pass with Science & Math. Min 35% marks required for most branches.

2

Fill Online Application

Apply through BCECE Board or direct at college admission office.

3

Merit-Based Selection

Seats allotted based on 10th merit. Counseling at BCECE Board, Patna.

4

Document Verification & Fee

Submit documents at college and pay nominal semester fee to confirm admission.
