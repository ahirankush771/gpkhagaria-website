// ADMISSION

How to Apply — 2025–26

Admissions are processed through Bihar DCECE. Follow these steps to secure your seat.

01

Register for DCECE

Visit bceceboard.bihar.gov.in and register for the Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination.

02

Appear for Examination

Take the DCECE exam (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) as per the scheduled date.

03

Check Merit List

Results and merit rank list are published on the official BCECE board website.

04

Document Verification

Report to GP Khagaria with original documents for verification and seat confirmation.

05

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the tuition fee and complete enrollment to begin your academic journey.
